Chocolate Peanut Butter Banana Smoothie

Description

This smoothie is ideal for weight training, assisting in gaining muscle and weight. It is effective in its purpose and delicous.

Ingredients

  • Milk
  • Banana
  • Peanut butter
  • Whey protein

Steps

  1. Pour 400 Ml of milk into your blender
  2. Put a banana into the blender.
  3. Drop two large scoops of peanut butter into the blender.
  4. Drop in chocolate flavored whey protein into the blender. The amount of whey is up to you.
  5. Blend for two minutes
  6. After the smoothie finished blending pour it into a cup and enjoy!

This smoothie, should have atleast 30 grams of protein depending on how much whey you put in. This is a great amount for weight training goals or if you're just hungry it will fill you right up with good nutrition.
